FastStack

Version: 0.3.1

Features

  • High-speed Play
  • Customisable Keybindings
  • Customisable Engine Speed (up to 1000fps)
  • Multiple Randomizers and Rotation Systems
  • Replay and Highscore Support
  • Finesse Metrics
  • ARE and IHS/IRS support
  • Multiple frontends
  • Windows, Linux and an os-independent x86 iso image
